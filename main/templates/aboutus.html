A long journey indeed


While registering for the event no one would have thought that this event would last so long.
Having qualified for idea submission, we were very excited, a lot of ideas popped into our mind, some seemingly difficult some easy, members put up a lot of ideas on the table, one said maybe we can develop a software that would find the safest path to travel from a source to destination, the other that maybe we could develop an automated drone that could tackle an enemy drone swarm, one will admit that ideas were diverse, at a point we were considering to build a catapult powered drone for last-mile delivery of amazon packets. We were ambitious indeed.

Lastly, we decided to build an application that would simply ”Turn off the light”. What does a man desire when he is so tired that he falls asleep? that someone turns that bulb off. This stuck to us as a great idea. Something that has novelty and nobility in it. We believe that this seemingly simple idea can make a lot of difference in the life of people who suffer from nyctophobia or similar disorders.
We are proud of being able to develop a prototype of our application.

Thankyou note Yes..

But we were able to sail through all these challenges and were finally able to complete this project.
